I'm Dinbandhu Panjayara, a Full Stack Developer


With experience in all parts of project development, I can turn your ideas into fully functional applications. I focus on web development and work with technologies such as C#, C++, Python, Blazor, React.js, Vue.js, and other Microsoft tools. My goal is to create user-friendly applications that meet client needs and exceed expectations.


I have worked extensively with various technologies. My expertise includes C#, C++, React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Next.js, Nuxt.js, Blazor (both WebAssembly and server-side), and various UI component libraries such as Razor Class Libraries and Components, Radzen, MudBlazor, and Syncfusion and DevExpress. I excel in architecture design, database design, both frontend and backend development, testing, deployment, and ongoing maintenance. My project management skills enable me to lead teams and ensure that projects are completed on time and within budget. I am dedicated to continuous learning and adapting to new technologies to provide the best solutions for my clients.


I have had the privilege of working across various domains, including stock and trading, supply chain management, healthcare systems, point of sale and eCommerce, CRM and ERP systems, compliance management, warehouse and inventory management, and B2B and B2C platforms. My experience in dashboard reporting and analysis has equipped me to deliver insights that drive business decisions.


At Clickvalley Technologies OPC Private Limited, I handled various tasks that prepared me to tackle new challenges. My responsibilities included collaborating with cross-functional teams to design and implement robust software solutions. I am responsible, hardworking, and capable of working independently with minimal supervision. I believe in maintaining a positive attitude and fostering a collaborative environment to achieve common goals.
